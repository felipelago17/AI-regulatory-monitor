📚 How This Works

Impact Ratings & Scoring

Each regulatory item receives a Risk Score (0-100) based on the Hubbard Calibration methodology—a quantified approach to uncertainty reduction:

  • Probability: % likelihood of impact occurring
  • Financial Impact: Low, Likely, and High estimates ($M)
  • Reputational Risk: Potential damage to brand/operations
  • Regulatory Disruption: Days of operational downtime

⚠️ Important: These are estimates for triage, not predictions of certainty. Always consult original regulatory sources and legal counsel.

Search & Filter Features

  • Search: Finds keywords in titles, summaries, and tags across all items
  • Jurisdiction: Filter by regulatory region (US, EU, UK, UAE, etc.)
  • Topic: Filter by regulatory domain (Export Controls, Data Privacy, AI Governance)
  • Source: Filter by the regulatory body or publication
  • Quick Views: All, Enforcements (penalties), Updates (new rules), News, High Risk (65+)

Visualizations

  • Sparklines: 30-day trend of activity (under KPI cards)
  • Enforcement Heatmap: Activity intensity over 365 days
  • Jurisdiction Map: Risk distribution by regulatory region
  • Timeline: Chronological view of regulatory events
  • Risk Bubble Chart: Probability vs Financial Impact (in AI Insights)

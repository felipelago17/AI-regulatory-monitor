# Data Directory — Governance Signals

This directory contains structured governance signals tracked by the AI Regulatory Monitor.

Signals stored here represent authoritative or influential developments relevant to AI governance, including:

- Binding regulations (laws, delegated acts)
- Regulatory guidance and soft law
- Enforcement actions
- Company AI policies
- Corporate governance statements and blogs

## Design Principles

- Signals are stored as structured data (not free text)
- Factual summaries are kept separate from interpretive analysis
- Each signal has a clear source and update date
- The structure supports later automation without losing context

## Status

At this stage, data ingestion is manual and curated.
Automation may be introduced later once structures stabilize.
